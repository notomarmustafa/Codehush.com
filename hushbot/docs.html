Features

This is the features section

Admin Tools

  • Role Management: Easily add or remove roles for users, helping manage permissions within your server.
  • Moderation Tools: Ban, kick, and unban users with simple commands to maintain a safe community.
  • Message Logging: Automatically log user actions for better oversight and accountability.

Fun Games

  • Trivia and Quizzes: Engage your server members with fun trivia questions and quizzes.
  • Games: Play interactive games like Hangman, Rock-Paper-Scissors, and more to enhance community interaction.
  • Meme Responses: Get random memes in response to specific keywords for some light-hearted fun.

Music Commands

  • Play Music: Join voice channels and play your favorite songs.
  • Manage Queue: Easily add, skip, or pause songs in your listening queue.
  • Resume and Leave: Control your music experience seamlessly.

Greeting Features

  • Welcome Messages: Automatically greet new members and make them
  • Goodbye Messages: Send personalized farewell messages to departing members.

Server Stats

  • Real-Time Member Stats: Display voice channels with real-time stats on total members, online members, and bot members.
  • Customizable Views: Easily manage what statistics are displayed and who can see them.

User Friendly Interface

  • Slash Commands: Utilize intuitive slash commands for a smoother user experience.
  • Custom Commands: Create and manage custom commands tailored to your server's needs.

Engagement & Community Building

  • Encourages Interaction: Features that promote member engagement and foster community bonding.
  • Flexible Configurations: Adjust settings and commands to suit your server's unique atmosphere.
